How It Works

Simple for everyone in the community.

Whether you're buying, selling, or just helping a neighbor — BringMe.Store makes it easy.

01
Search the Map
Open BringMe.Store and see listings, stores, and help requests pinned on a real map of your area. Filter by category or drag the radius slider.
02
Post a Listing or Help Request
Selling something? Type the product name and our database auto-fills the details. Need something urgently? Post a Help Request and neighbors reply with store locations.
03
Connect & Deal
View contact info, call the store, or get directions — all inside the app. Deals happen your way: meet up, GCash, or delivery.
04
Earn Points for Helping
Reply to Help Requests with store locations. When the requester confirms you helped — you earn ⭐ 1 point = ₱1, cashable to GCash or Maya.

Features

Everything your community needs.

Built specifically for Filipino communities — local, fast, and free to use.

🗺️
Real Map with Live Pins
Every listing, store, and help request is pinned on a real OpenStreetMap. Adjust your search radius from 0.5km to 20km with a slider.
🚨
Urgent Find
Need something right now? Post a Help Request and the community directs you to the nearest store that has it — in minutes.
🔍
Smart Product Search
Start typing a product name when posting — our database auto-fills the title, category, description, and suggested price. Powered by FakeStore & Open Food Facts APIs.
⭐
Points = Real Cash
Help neighbors find stores and earn points. Every 1 point = ₱1, cashable to GCash or Maya. No minimum. Cash out anytime.

Store Directory
Stores register themselves on the map with their products, hours, and contact. Buyers find exactly what they need without calling around.

Stores and sellers can boost their visibility with plans starting at ₱99/week — appearing at the top of search results and highlighted on the map.
